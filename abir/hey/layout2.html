Payments

How can I make a payment?

You can make a payment using credit/debit cards, online payment gateways like Bkash or Nogod, or your wallet balance.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, refunds are processed based on our cancellation policy. Please contact support for more details.

Bookings

How do I book a slot?

Navigate to the slot booking section, select an available time slot, and confirm your booking.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel a booking from your account's reservation details section.

Account Management

How can I update my profile information?

Go to the account settings section and update your profile details like email, phone number, or address.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.
